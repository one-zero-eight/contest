Why do I need to log in to my Google account to fill the form?

We use the Google Drive file upload service, it requires your credentials. Also, we kindly ask you include your Gmails with your responses for moderation purposes. Don't worry, we are not going to disclose your sensitive data to public.

How to draw?

1. Choose a model on the main page (or on this page, or in the provided sources).
2. Download the image hint, or the mask image, or all together.
3. In your favourite soft, create a texture and distribute it according guides.
4. Test your textures in 3D Applier by choosing them in the Apply button's popup window.

Will my work be signed in the Gallery?

No, we are not going to disclose authors there, but every work is signed with a numeric id. We display it near the # character.
