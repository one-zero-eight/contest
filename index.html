We announce a design contestfrom 108

And propose you to participate in developing our new merch

Technical task

You are provided with a couple of template 3D models of our merch things without textures

Also you will have special images that will serve you as w/b texture masks. Based on them, you need to create texture images using any software you want and apply it to our models.

Here we provide resources you might use.
You can get more information on how to draw on the 3D Applier page,
as well as test your textures.

Rules

    • You are going to draw a square texture for our templates of supposed set of merch
      Do NOT change unwrapping of the models
      3D Applier is a web interface, where you can interact with our 3d models using the mouse, change their textures and get hints, but if you want, you are still able to use other services
    • Casual and light style is welcome
      Do NOT use Innopolis property, brands, and trademarks. Or do it very carefully, stilization is better
      It would be great if you provide source files if you work in vector graphics
      Your submitted work has to be compatible with Creative Commons 0
      The winner will be chosen based on the opinion of one-zero-eight and, maybe, yours, follow us @one-zero-eight
